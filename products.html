Our Products

Illuminating Innovation, Powering Excellence

At Shruti Electricals, we are dedicated to manufacturing top-quality electrical products while providing comprehensive support and service. With Shruti Electricals, you can trust that your projects are in expert hands, allowing you to focus on what matters most while we deliver excellence and reliability in every product and service we offer.

LED
Bulb

Our energy-efficient LED bulbs provide superior lighting while significantly reducing energy consumption. They offer long-lasting performance and are available in various wattages and color temperatures to suit different applications, from residential to commercial spaces.

Tube
Light

Designed for optimal brightness and efficiency, our tube lights are ideal for illuminating large areas. They provide even, flicker-free lighting, making them perfect for offices, schools, and industrial environments. Our tube lights are durable and easy to install, ensuring reliable performance over time.

Panel
Light

Our sleek and modern panel lights offer excellent illumination with a minimalist design. These lights are perfect for ceilings in homes, offices, and retail spaces, providing a clean, contemporary look while delivering uniform light distribution. They are energy-efficient and have a long lifespan, making them a cost-effective lighting solution.

Sideways/Walkways Bulb

Engineered for outdoor use, our sideways and walkways bulbs ensure safety and visibility in pathways, gardens, and other exterior spaces. They are designed to withstand weather conditions while providing bright, consistent light. These bulbs enhance outdoor aesthetics and improve security with their reliable performance.
